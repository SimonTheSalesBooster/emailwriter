## What It Does

Once installed, `/emailwriter` gives Claude deep knowledge of:

- **Copywriting frameworks** — AIDA, PAS, BAB, Soap Opera Sequences, Sprint Openers, Preeminence positioning
- **Automation flows** — Welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back, and 8 more ranked by revenue impact
- **Cold email** — Infrastructure setup, warming schedules, personalisation levels, follow-up cadences
- **Deliverability** — SPF/DKIM/DMARC, domain warming, Gmail/Outlook 2025-2026 changes, 10-step diagnosis
- **19 industry playbooks** — Ecommerce, SaaS, Newsletter, Nonprofit, B2B, Coaching, and 13 more
- **Benchmarks** — Open rates, CTR, reply rates, RPR by industry and email type
- **Compliance** — CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, Australian Spam Act

Every email Claude writes comes with subject line alternatives for A/B testing, preview text, and notes on which frameworks were applied.

## Usage

```
/emailwriter <describe what you need>
```

### Examples

#### Cold outreach for SaaS founders
```
/emailwriter cold email for SaaS founders who need more pipeline
```
Generates a 50-125 word cold email using the Sprint Opener framework — specific observation, gap identification, bounded 90-day timeframe, soft CTA. Plus 3 subject line variants and a 4-email follow-up structure.

#### Welcome series for a coaching business
```
/emailwriter welcome series for a coaching business
```
Designs a 4-6 email sequence over 14 days — deliver the lead magnet, tell the origin story, stack social proof, segment by interest, soft-sell the core offer. Each email with subject lines, timing, and framework notes.

#### Abandoned cart recovery for DTC ecommerce
```
/emailwriter abandoned cart sequence for DTC ecommerce
```
Builds a 3-email recovery flow: reminder at 1-4h (no discount), objection handling at 24h (reviews + shipping + guarantee), incentive at 48h (first-time abandoners only). Targets 17%+ conversion rate.

#### Win-back for lapsed members
```
/emailwriter win-back email for lapsed Sprint Club members
```
Creates a win-back sequence targeting 60-90 day inactive members — "we miss you" opener, value reminder, breakup email (2-3x reply rate), and re-subscribe confirmation. Uses the Preeminence framework to lead with genuine value.
