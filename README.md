## Show me

### Cold outreach for SaaS founders

```
/emailwriter cold email for SaaS founders who need more pipeline
```

You get a 50-125 word cold email using the Sprint Opener framework... specific observation, gap identification, bounded 90-day timeframe, soft CTA. Plus 3 subject line variants and a 4-email follow-up structure.

### Welcome series for a coaching business

```
/emailwriter welcome series for a coaching business
```

A 4-6 email sequence over 14 days... deliver the lead magnet, tell the origin story, stack social proof, segment by interest, soft-sell the core offer. Each email with subject lines, timing, and framework notes.

### Abandoned cart recovery

```
/emailwriter abandoned cart sequence for DTC ecommerce
```

3-email recovery flow: reminder at 1-4h (no discount), objection handling at 24h (reviews + shipping + guarantee), incentive at 48h (first-time abandoners only). Targets 17%+ conversion rate.

### Win-back for lapsed members

```
/emailwriter win-back email for lapsed Sprint Club members
```

Win-back sequence targeting 60-90 day inactive members... "we miss you" opener, value reminder, breakup email (2-3x reply rate), and re-subscribe confirmation. Uses the Preeminence framework to lead with genuine value.
